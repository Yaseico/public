About

What we do

Our simple mission is to help individuals and families imrove their experience with modern technology, the internet and importantly keep their digital infomation and accounts (ie digital assests) both private and secure. Managing these assets while you use them and beyond requires a little bit of thought. We help with this using simple to understand guides, videos and trainingonline courses. Personal and famility use of our resources is free of any charges. Access and use by commercial and registered busines is subject to a paid subscription.

Family privacy and security when using the internet

Just about everyone who is able uses the internet. We help outline some best practices and provide information and resources to help raise and maintain awareneess of good and bad practices.

Artificial intelligence

AI can be used for positive outcomes and also result in catastrophe. It is rightly both exiting and a concern. We aim to explain in simle terms and privide some guidelines to help individuals and families explore this merging technology

Personal Tech and tech in the home

Our homes are connected to the internet, with many smart devices, cameras, TV's and computers. Many people have smart wearable tech and use smart mobile phones. We provide resources and tools to help explain risks and provide information and resources on best practices
